I wish I could make you feel the depth of my love for you.
Words will fail to show my eternal love for you.
You are my world, my everything.
Your smile lights up my world. You rule my heart;
you made my life amazing. I feel so happy and proud when
I call you “mine.” Your love makes me feel alive.
I am not a possessive husband, but I won’t let anyone
stare at my girl for more than 0.001 seconds.
I cherish everything about you.
I never felt this way before the way I feel for you.
You are my love, my happiness, and my strength.
I want to spend the rest of my life holding your hands.
